How to Play Online

Connect with your friends in three simple steps.

01

Create/Join Room

Open Multiplayer mode, configure your profile, and choose to "Host" a new room or "Join" an existing one.

02

Share Room Code

Share the generated 6-digit room code with your friend. They enter it on their device to establish a direct connection.

03

Flip & Play!

The animated coin toss decides who takes the first turn. Place your X or O and block your opponent to win!
