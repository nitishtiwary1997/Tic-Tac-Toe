Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Tic Tac Toe: Online & Local ("Application") for mobile devices, which was created as a clean, offline-friendly, and online matchmaking multiplayer board game.

1. Information Collection and Use

We believe in absolute transparency and user privacy. The Application is designed so that you can play without registering an account or providing any personally identifiable information (PII).

A. Offline & Local Play Modes

When playing in Player vs AI or Local Pass & Play modes:

  • No personal data is collected or transmitted outside of your device.
  • Custom nicknames, selected avatars, board settings, and game statistics are stored locally on your device using Android Jetpack DataStore (Preferences).

B. Online Multiplayer Mode

When you choose to play in Online Multiplayer mode, the Application uses third-party cloud services to synchronize the gameplay in real-time. In this mode, we process:

  • Room Codes: A temporary, randomly generated 6-digit number to connect you with your game partner.
  • Temporary Nicknames & Avatars: The player names and avatar emojis you choose to identify yourself in the game room.
  • Game Actions: The coordinates of the squares you tap on the 3x3 board during the game session.

Note: All online match information is transient and stored temporarily to facilitate active matchmaking. We do not link this data to your personal identity, device identifiers, or social accounts.

2. Third-Party Services

To enable online multiplayer features and match synchronization, the Application utilizes service providers that may collect information used to identify you. You can read the privacy policies of these providers below:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (Realtime Database / Firestore)

3. Device Permissions Required

The Application requests the following basic permissions on your device to function correctly:

  • INTERNET: Required to establish connection to Firebase servers for online multiplayer matchmaking and real-time syncing.
  • VIBRATE: Used to offer haptic feedback (vibrations) on grid interactions and victory celebrations, enhancing immersion.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE & ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: Used to check if your device has an active internet connection before starting online rooms, ensuring a smooth experience.

4. Data Retention and Safety

Because we do not collect personal identifiers or maintain user accounts, we do not store your personal information on any database. All local preferences (like stats, volume, theme, and colors) are stored directly on your physical device. You can erase this data at any time by clearing the Application's cache/storage in your device's settings or by uninstalling the Application.

5. Children's Privacy

The Application is fully suitable for children and does not knowingly collect or request personal information from children under the age of 13. The game contains no advertisements, chat features, or in-app purchases, making it a safe environment for all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
