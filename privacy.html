Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe: Online & Local ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to username/nickname (stored locally on your device or transmitted for online multiplayer session matching). The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by us in any personal database.

The App does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The link to the privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics

3. Game Data & Multiplayer

When you play online using multiplayer codes, temporary session data (including game board state and your chosen nickname/avatar) is processed securely through our real-time synchronization servers solely to run the live match. This session data is temporary and is deleted once the multiplayer session ends.

Your lifetime game statistics, settings, theme preferences, and custom nickname are stored locally on your device via Android shared preferences. We do not upload this local profile data to any external server.

4. Log Data (Errors & Crashes)

We want to inform you that whenever you use our App, in case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products like Firebase Crashlytics) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics. This is used solely to debug and improve app stability.

5. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory. This Service does not use these “cookies” explicitly.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do the necessary actions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:

Email: support@smart.ntictactoe.com
